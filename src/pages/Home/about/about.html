About Us Joynal is a "happiness diary", a place to record moments, events and thoughts that have made you happy in the past 24 hours.

Keeping a happiness diary and focusing on the positive things that have happened to you is proven to boost serotonin and keep you feeling happier, stronger and in good mental health. Joynal allows you to do this easily and at any time using your phone and helps keep you motivated with Streaks and Achievements.
